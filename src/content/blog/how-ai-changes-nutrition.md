## The Future of Calorie Tracking

We've all been there: standing in front of your lunch, supposed to type every single ingredient into an app. Weigh the rice, guess the vegetables, ignore the sauce. After three days, you're done.

**That's why 60% of all users quit tracking their nutrition within the first week.**

## Photo Instead of Typing

What if you could just take a photo? That's exactly what Flexen's AI food recognition does. The technology behind it:

- **Computer Vision** identifies food on your plate
- **Portion estimation** calculates approximate amounts
- **Nutrition database** delivers calories and macros in real time

The result: Instead of 5 minutes per meal, you need 5 seconds.

## Accuracy vs. Speed

Sure, an AI isn't perfect. But it doesn't have to be. Studies show that 85-90% accurate estimation is sufficient for most fitness goals. The decisive advantage: you actually track, instead of giving up because it's too tedious.

> "The best tracking method is the one you actually use."
